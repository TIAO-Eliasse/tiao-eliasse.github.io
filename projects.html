🏦 BANKING · PREDICTIVE MAINTENANCE · Apr–Jun 2025

Predictive ATM monitoring — Afriland First Bank

Why it matters: ATM downtime directly loses revenue. 1% uptime gain = 7–10% cost reduction over 12 months per machine.

Structured raw ATM event logs into a predictive dataset with temporal, technical and location features; benchmarked five model families to anticipate prolonged outages within a 2–4 day horizon.

✓ F1 = 82% ✓ AUC = 95% ✓ +7.19 pts uptime (simulated)

📊 ENTERPRISE · SURVIVAL PREDICTION · Mar–Jul 2025

SME survival early-warning system — National Institute of Statistics

Why it matters: 80% of African SMEs fail in 5 years. Early detection allows intervention; dashboard now guides policy decisions.

Built a longitudinal dataset by linking enterprise census records with fiscal data; combined logistic regression, ensemble ML and survival modelling to identify failure drivers and route high-risk firms for support.

✓ CatBoost AUC = 0.884 ✓ RSF C-index = 0.70 ✓ Deployed dashboard
