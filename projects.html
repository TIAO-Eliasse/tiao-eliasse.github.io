BANKING · PREDICTIVE MAINTENANCE · Apr–Jun 2025

Predictive ATM monitoring — Afriland First Bank

Structured raw ATM event logs into a predictive dataset with temporal, technical and location features; benchmarked Random Forest, XGBoost, CatBoost, stacking and neural classifiers to anticipate prolonged outages 2–4 days ahead.

F1 = 82% · AUC = 95% · simulations suggest +7.19 pts machine availability

Random ForestXGBoostFeature engineeringBanking

ENTERPRISE RISK · SURVIVAL MODELLING · Mar–Jul 2025

SME survival early-warning system — National Institute of Statistics

Built a longitudinal SME dataset by linking enterprise census records with fiscal data; combined logistic regression, ensemble ML and survival modelling to identify drivers of business cessation, deployed to a policy dashboard.

CatBoost AUC = 0.884 · F1 = 79.8% · RSF C-index = 0.70 · paper accepted (Indaba/IJCAI)

CatBoostSurvival analysisEconometricsStreamlit

ENERGY · PROBABILISTIC FORECASTING · Feb–Jul 2026

Explainable energy forecasting — Wattzo (Germany, remote)

Uncertainty-aware framework jointly modelling electricity demand and solar PV production across 12 commercial and industrial sites; gradient-boosting ensembles benchmarked against CNN–Transformer quantile models, with a Peak-Window conformal calibration strategy.

Calibrated prediction intervals in high-demand regimes · SHAP diagnostics on interval width & coverage
