What I've learned

On research

Uncertainty quantification isn't an afterthought—it's foundational. Conformal prediction and Bayesian approaches scale better than post-hoc calibration. Explainability through feature attribution (SHAP) and uncertainty decomposition earns trust from operators and policymakers.

On deployment

Real systems need deferral strategies. Not every prediction should be automated—humans should route uncertain cases. Cost-aware thresholds and fairness constraints beat simple probability cutoffs.
