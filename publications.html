FloodTriage: A Decision-Theoretic Tri-Fold Deferral Policy Towards Human-in-the-Loop Streamflow Forecasting

Gohouedé, L.C., Gohouedé, M.A.L., TIAO, E., Edou, J.R.

Impact: Reduces false flood alarms while maintaining 100% miss detection. Routes only 25% of predictions to humans for review in resource-constrained settings.

Human-AI collaboration framework for flood forecasting on the Ouémé River (Cameroon). LSTM deep ensemble governed by tri-fold deferral gateway that decomposes decisions into epistemic, aleatoric, and Knightian uncertainty signals.
